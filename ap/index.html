Why It Matters

Why take AP courses?

AP is the fastest way to build a stronger university application — and save real money once you're admitted.

Earn College Credit

Score 3, 4, or 5 and skip equivalent courses at 4,000+ universities. That's one fewer tuition bill.

Stronger Application

US & Korean admissions offices explicitly reward students who challenge themselves with AP. It signals academic readiness.

Scholarship Impact

Many scholarship committees look at AP participation. It demonstrates you can handle university-level work before arriving.

Exam Discipline

AP teaches you to perform under real exam conditions. Students who pass AP consistently score higher on SAT too.
