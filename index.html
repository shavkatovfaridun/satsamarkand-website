FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What happens if I miss a class?
Every class is recorded or notes are shared — you won't fall behind for missing one session. If you miss multiple classes, we'll schedule a make-up session with your mentor. We track attendance and proactively reach out if we notice you're falling behind.
How much does SAT prep cost in Samarkand?
SAT Samarkand offers three programs: Intensive (from 700,000 UZS/month), SAT MAX with 1500+ guarantee (from 1,000,000 UZS/month), and Private 1-on-1 (from 2,000,000 UZS/month). Discounts are available when enrolling for both Math and English.
How long does it take to prepare for the SAT?
Most students need 3 to 6 months of focused preparation. Students starting below 1100 typically need 6 months. Students above 1200 can often reach 1500+ in 3 months with the right preparation. Your diagnostic test result will give you a personalized recommendation.
Is there a free SAT test in Samarkand?
Yes — two options. First, take the free online diagnostic test at diagnostic.satsamarkand.uz. Second, attend our free Sunday Mock SAT at Temur Malik 19 every Sunday at 10:00 AM. Both are completely free with no commitment required.
Where is SAT Samarkand located?
SAT Samarkand has two locations in Samarkand, Uzbekistan: Main center at Temur Malik 19, and a second branch at Joylink Academy on Mirzo Ulugbek 105. Both locations are open 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM daily.
